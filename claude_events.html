Build-a-Bridge Challenge

Unami Middle School
3-Day Event · 50 min per session

Students compete to build the strongest bridge using 25 wooden sticks, 1 meter of string, 1 sheet of cardboard, and unlimited tape/glue — all in 50 minutes. The event spans three days: Day 1 for planning, Day 2 for building, and Day 3 for testing with weights. Winners receive gift cards and Unami Middle School merch!

Open to Unami Middle School Students Only
